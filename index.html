Why You Should Use Tonfa

Considered the blunt dualblades, Tonfas are a fast hit weapon that requires high skill to be able to use correctly. Aerial being faster and having a total of 3 different dodges, is the best choice in order to do damage and fly around the monster. Ground being slower but incredibly satisfying with his ground ex evades + charged moves. Down here you have the two guides I have made, so you can learn the basics of the weapon. Have fun with Tonfa!!!!
